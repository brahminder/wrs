Temporary Radio Journalist

 

World Radio Switzerland is the English-speaking radio station of the Swiss Broadcasting Corporation. It is currently being privatized, a process that will take between 12 and 16 months. WRS is looking for a Radio Journalist for a 3½ month contract.

.

THE POSITION

You will read/write news bulletins, conduct interviews and produce news/feature reports. You will participate in the daily editorial meeting and propose story ideas.

 

YOUR QUALIFICATIONS

• Degree in journalism or equivalent

  • • At least two years of solid radio experience.
  • • Strong editorial skills and judgment
  • • Audio editing and production skills (we use Adobe Audition)
  • • Solid grasp of news and current affairs.
  • • Fluency in French or German is required; second national language is an advantage
  • • Knowledge of Switzerland, its institutions, geography and economic actors is an advantage
  • • Capable of working independently and as part of a team, but under minimum supervision
  • • Ability to work under pressure and to deadline
  • • Flexible, open-minded and ability to assimilate information quickly
  • • Swiss or European citizenship

 

WHAT WE CAN OFFER

• To work with a maximum of autonomy in a goal oriented environment

  • • An exciting opportunity to leverage your skills in an international environment
  • • An excellent financial package

Position                  Short-term contract (3 ½ months)

Location                 Geneva

Period                     15 Apr – 30 Aug 2013

Application             Deadline 22 Feb 2013

We will only consider applications sent via the website “Emplois” on www.rts.ch (application with detailed CV, including references, and a comprehensive cover letter). Only candidates selected for interview will be contacted. No phone inquiries.
