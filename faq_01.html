      How do I listen to WRS online?

      To listen to WRS streaming live on the website, look for the orange "Listen Online"link at the top right of every page. To listen via iTunes or other Internet radio applications, copy and paste the following URL into the client of your choosing: http://provisioning. streamtheworld.com/pls/WRS.pls

      How can I listen to WRS?

      World Radio Switzerland is available in the Geneva region on 101.7 FM. For listeners in Switzerland outside of Geneva, WRS is available nationwide on DAB+ radio. Cable and satellite subscribers throughout the country can also listen to us via music channels on these services. Listeners in neighboring countries just across the border may also be able to pick up WRS via DAB+. Or you can listen to us anytime via live streaming on worldradio.ch. We have no FM frequency in German-speaking Switzerland.

      Where can I buy a DAB + radio?

      DAB+ is the best way to listen to WRS across Switzerland. A wide range of DAB+ receivers is available at most electronics stores in Switzerland. You can shop online and compare prices here (NOTE: Make sure it is a DAB+ radio and not the older DAB standard): In-car systems Portable DAB+ radios Buy online

      Does WRS broadcast advertising?

      As WRS is a public service broadcaster under Swiss laws and regulations, radio commercials are not allowed, however sponsorships of shows and segments on the air are available. If you are interested in sponsoring our shows or segments, please contact our sales staff at wrs@publisuisse.ch.
